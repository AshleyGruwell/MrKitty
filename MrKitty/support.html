Volunteer

All potential volunteers are required to submit a completed application and attend a volunteer orientation before joining our team.
A nonrefundable $20 orientation fee covers the cost of your T-shirt and class materials. Certain tasks or positions may necessitate additional training.
If you're under 18, parental consent is required. Ages 10-15 must have a supervising adult volunteer, while those under 10 will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
